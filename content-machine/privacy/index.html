Content Machine Legal

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2026

Content Machine is an AI-powered autonomous content operating system created and operated by Cenk Emir Bat (“we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains what information Content Machine collects, how it is used, how it is protected, and the choices available to you.

01 What Content Machine Does

Content Machine is a personal AI-powered system that generates short-form video content, creates scripts automatically, produces and schedules videos, publishes content to connected social media accounts, and collects performance analytics to improve future content strategies. Currently supported platforms are YouTube Shorts and TikTok.

02 Information We Collect

When you connect social media accounts via OAuth, we may collect or access:

  • Account identifiers (user IDs, channel IDs, creator account identifiers).
  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, upload status, video IDs).
  • Publishing information (scheduled publish times, visibility settings, platform-assigned URLs).
  • Performance analytics (view counts, watch time, engagement rates, impressions, click-through rates).
  • Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, saves, follower changes).

We do not collect payment information, browsing history unrelated to published content, or personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

03 OAuth Authentication

Content Machine uses OAuth 2.0 to connect to social media platforms including YouTube and TikTok. When you authorize Content Machine via OAuth:

  • You grant Content Machine the specific permissions required to publish content and retrieve analytics on your behalf.
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely and used solely to perform actions you have authorized.
  • You may revoke Content Machine’s access at any time through the platform’s own settings (for example, Google Account permissions or TikTok app settings).
  • Revoking OAuth access does not automatically delete previously collected analytics data. You may request deletion separately (see Section 8).

04 How We Use Information

Information accessed through connected accounts is used exclusively to:

  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts as scheduled.
  • Retrieve performance analytics for published content.
  • Analyze content performance to improve future content recommendations, scheduling decisions, and content generation quality.
  • Maintain the functionality, security, and reliability of the system.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling for third-party purposes, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of Content Machine.

05 Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information or analytics data to any third party.

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • As necessary to deliver the service (for example, using platform APIs to publish content — data is sent to the platform you have authorized).
  • If required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the operator or others, where permitted by law.

06 Data Retention

We retain collected analytics and account data only as long as necessary to provide the service and improve content performance. If you disconnect your account or request deletion of your data, your data will be removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by applicable law.

07 Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration. OAuth tokens are stored securely and are never exposed publicly. However, no system can guarantee absolute security, and you use Content Machine at your own risk.

08 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR and similar regulations) to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Request restriction of processing.
  • Object to processing of your data.
  • Request portability of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 11. We will respond to verifiable requests within 30 days or the timeframe required by applicable law.

09 International Data Transfers

Data may be processed and stored in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, including countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your location. By using Content Machine or authorizing OAuth access, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred internationally in connection with providing the service.

10 Third-Party Platform Policies

Content Machine integrates with YouTube (operated by Google LLC) and TikTok. Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective terms of service and privacy policies, which are independent of this policy. Content Machine’s access to these platforms is limited to the permissions you explicitly grant via OAuth.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The effective date above will be updated accordingly. Continued use of Content Machine after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
